# Annotation Agreement Plan v1.0

> **Ngày:** 17/07/2026
> **Owner:** C — Annotation & Data Quality
> **Trạng thái:** Measurement plan only; no agreement metric/result exists.

## 1. Sampling

Independently double-review 10–20% of available annotation records, stratified where possible by scenario, positive/negative, interference and edge condition. Reviewers should be blinded to the other review when practical.

## 2. Measurements

- percent agreement for label, positive/negative and ambiguous/invalid status;
- Cohen’s Kappa for two reviewers or Fleiss’ Kappa for more than two, if sample design permits;
- absolute start/end boundary discrepancy in raw-source seconds;
- disagreement rate by label and condition.

Empty/negative clips and small sample sizes must be reported as limitations. Results are diagnostic until the release criterion is approved.
